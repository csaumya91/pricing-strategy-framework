# Pricing Problem Structure

Pricing decisions must balance three forces:

customer willingness to pay

competitive positioning

internal margin constraints


## Core Pricing Questions

Is this customer price-sensitive?

Is this deal expansion or retention-driven?

What competitive pressure exists?

What margin floor must be protected?

What long-term value does this account represent?


## Pricing Decision Inputs

customer size

contract tenure

region

competitive intensity

strategic importance

volume commitment

growth potential


These inputs guide pricing customization decisions.
