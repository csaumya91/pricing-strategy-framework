# Pricing Strategy Framework

## Overview

This project demonstrates a structured pricing strategy framework used to improve margin quality, support enterprise deal structuring, and guide competitive positioning and strategy decisions.

The framework enables pricing teams to evaluate:

customer segmentation
competitive positioning
margin sensitivity
deal customization logic
risk-adjusted pricing scenarios

It is designed as a reusable decision-support structure for revenue and strategy teams.

---

## Business Problem

Pricing decisions often suffer from:

inconsistent discounting logic
lack of segmentation-based pricing
limited visibility into margin trade-offs
reactive competitor matching

This framework introduces a structured approach to improve pricing consistency and decision quality.

---

## Framework Components

Customer Segmentation Model

Margin Sensitivity Analysis

Competitive Benchmarking Logic

Deal Structuring Decision Tree

Scenario-Based Pricing Recommendations

enterprise contract renewal optimization
