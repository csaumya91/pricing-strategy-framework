# Customer Segmentation Model

Customers are segmented based on pricing sensitivity and strategic value.

Segment 1: Strategic Enterprise Accounts

high contract value,
multi-year engagement,
low churn tolerance

Pricing approach:

value-based pricing,
controlled discounting,
long-term margin optimization


Segment 2: Growth Accounts

medium contract value,
high expansion opportunity

Pricing approach:

incentivized entry pricing,
structured upsell path


Segment 3: Price-Sensitive Accounts

high competition exposure,
low switching friction

Pricing approach:

competitive parity pricing,
volume-linked incentives


Segment 4: Transactional Customers

short-term engagement,
limited strategic importance

Pricing approach:

standardized pricing tiers,
automation-driven discount logic
